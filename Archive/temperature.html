Max Temperature vs Degrees Latitude

From the Max Temperature vs Latitude scatter plot, it appears that the max temperature seems to increase from -40 degrees latitude to about 20 degrees latitude. From 20 degrees to 80 degrees, the max temperature decreases. There are a few cities that sort of break the trend, noticably the one at -30 latitude and ~105 degrees max temperature.
