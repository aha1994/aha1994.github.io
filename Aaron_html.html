Aaron's Top 5 Movies

A list (in no order) of my favorite movies and their trailers on Youtube.

  • 1. Shawshank Redemption
    Rating 9.2/10
    Description Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) is sentenced to two consecutive life terms in prison for the murders of his wife and her lover and is sentenced to a tough prison.
  • 2. Good Will Hunting
    Rating 8.2/10
    Description Will Hunting (Matt Damon) has a genius-level IQ but chooses to work as a janitor at MIT.
  • 3. Forrest Gump
    Rating 8.8/10
    Description Slow-witted Forrest Gump (Tom Hanks) has never thought of himself as disadvantaged, and thanks to his supportive mother (Sally Field), he leads anything but a restricted life.
  • 4. Finding Nemo
    Rating 8.1/10
    Description Marlin (Albert Brooks), a clown fish, is overly cautious with his son, Nemo (Alexander Gould), who has a foreshortened fin.
  • 5. Se7en
    Rating 8.6/10
    Description When retiring police Detective William Somerset (Morgan Freeman) tackles a final case with the aid of newly transferred David Mills (Brad Pitt), they discover a number of elaborate and grizzly murders.
